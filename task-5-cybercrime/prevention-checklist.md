### 1. Enable MFA: Activate Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) on your college email and all social media accounts.

### 2. Privacy Audit: Set your social media profiles to "Private" so only confirmed friends can see your posts.

### 3. Password Variety: Use a unique, complex password for every account; never reuse your college portal password elsewhere.

### 4. Secure Connections: Avoid logging into sensitive accounts (banking/Gmail) using public campus or cafe Wi-Fi.

### 5. Link Caution: Never click on "Account Verification" links received via SMS or unsolicited emails.

### 6. Software Updates: Keep your phone and laptop OS updated to ensure the latest security patches are active.

### 7. UPI Safety 1: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required for sending or checking balances.

### 8. UPI Safety 2: Verify the recipient's name on the UPI app before hitting 'Pay' to avoid "simulated" QR code scams.
